🔑 Use Strong Passwords

Use a combination of capital letters, numbers and symbols. Do not share your password with anyone.

📱 Protect Your Phone

Use screen locks and install apps only from trusted sources.

⚠ Avoid Fake Links

Do not click unknown links from emails or messages.

Safety Rules

  1. Create strong passwords
  2. Enable two-factor authentication
  3. Never share OTP or personal details
  4. Update software regularly
  5. Use secure websites

Good Online Habits

  • Logout from public computers
  • Use antivirus protection
  • Backup important files
  • Check privacy settings
