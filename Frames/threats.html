Types of Cyber Threats

Threat Description
🎣 Phishing Fake emails or websites used to steal personal information.
🦠 Malware Harmful software that damages computers and devices.
🔒 Ransomware Locks files and demands money to unlock them.
👤 Identity Theft Stealing personal details for illegal activities.

🎣 Phishing Attack

Attackers send fake messages to trick users.

🛡 Stay Protected

Be careful before sharing information online.
